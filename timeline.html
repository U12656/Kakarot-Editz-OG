📜 Dragon Ball Saga Timeline

Saiyan Saga

Raditz arrives on Earth revealing Goku is a Saiyan. Vegeta and Nappa attack Earth.

Namek Saga

The Z Fighters travel to Planet Namek to find the Dragon Balls while Frieza searches for immortality.

Frieza Saga

Goku transforms into Super Saiyan and defeats Frieza in an epic battle.

Android Saga

Future Trunks warns the Z Fighters about androids created by Dr. Gero.

Cell Saga

Cell creates the Cell Games tournament to fight Earth's strongest warriors.

Majin Buu Saga

The ancient monster Majin Buu awakens and threatens the universe.

Beerus Saga

The God of Destruction Beerus awakens and searches for the Super Saiyan God.

Golden Frieza Saga

Frieza returns stronger than ever with a new transformation: Golden Frieza.

Universe 6 Tournament Saga

Beerus and Champa organize a tournament between Universe 6 and Universe 7.

Future Trunks Saga

Future Trunks returns to fight the evil Goku Black and Zamasu.

Tournament of Power

Universe 7 battles other universes in a massive survival tournament.
